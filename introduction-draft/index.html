That amounted to a first cut at consolidating data across repos and log databases, and it shortened production-issue fix time by 22%.

我也曾因為自己的疏失導致 Live Revenue Matrix 下降，因此主持了一場 RCA meeting。最終催生出一套「當 feature 跨多個團隊時該有的協作流程」，並讓 AI 團隊立項了一個內部系統：它包含一道跨團隊的 QA 閘門、一個在開發前期就啟動的跨團隊討論機制，以及一套用 LLM 自動把異動指標關聯到可疑 feature 的內部系統。I also once caused a drop in the Live Revenue Matrix through my own oversight, so I ran an RCA meeting. 6我的代表作：一套每天在跑的 agentic AI 平台My Signature Work: An Agentic AI Platform That Runs Daily

同時我在工作之外，獨力從 0 到 1 打造並維運一套個人化 agentic AI 平台。一開始的動機很單純：我想充實自己在 AI agent 應用上的實戰知識，同時我也真的需要一套工具，能自動跨多個社群平台收集資料、經過一連串處理去蕪存菁，最後建成一個對 AI agent 友善、可搜尋的 RAG 知識庫。Outside of work, I single-handedly built and operate a personal agentic AI platform from 0 to 1. The motivation was simple: I wanted hands-on depth in AI-agent applications, and I genuinely needed a tool that could automatically gather data across multiple social platforms, run it through a pipeline that separates signal from noise, and build an agent-friendly, searchable RAG knowledge base.

這套平台現在有 33 個模組、串接了 3 家廠商的 LLM。它每天會自動從 3 個社群平台——Twitter、小紅書、YouTube——以及多家知名 AI 公司的技術部落格和官網，穩定爬進 200 篇以上的文章和影片（目前已穩定運行 1 個半月），爬下的內容再經過一條完整的 pipeline：媒體解析、分類評分、內容增強、到專家審查，最後沉澱成可搜尋的知識庫。The platform now has 33 modules and integrates LLMs from 3 vendors. Every day it automatically pulls 200+ articles and videos from 3 social platforms — Twitter, RED (Xiaohongshu) and YouTube — plus the tech blogs and sites of several well-known AI companies (it's run stably for 1.5 months). The captured content then flows through a full pipeline — media parsing, classification & scoring, content enrichment, and expert review — and settles into a searchable knowledge base.

過程中我也用 LLM 的 A/B test，在只損失 3% 解讀表現的前提下，把運行成本減少 83%。這整件事對我最大的意義，是它證明我真的能把一個模糊的 AI 想法，落地成可量測、可長期維運的系統。而我最深的體會是：要讓 AI 變成每天能用的東西，難的從來不是模型本身，而是它周邊的 pipeline、錯誤處理和成本控制——也就是把 AI「工程化」的那段功夫。Along the way I used LLM A/B testing to cut running cost by 83% while losing only 3% of interpretation quality. What this means most to me is that it proves I can take a fuzzy AI idea and land it as a measurable, long-running system. My deepest takeaway: making AI an everyday tool — the hard part was never the model itself, but the surrounding pipeline, error handling and cost control — the work of "engineering" AI.
